Biography

I am Research Director (equivalent to Full Professor) at CNRS (Department of Computer Science) and the founding co-director of the ARAMIS Lab, a joint team between CNRS, Inria, Inserm and Sorbonne University at the Paris Brain Institute (ICM). I hold a Chair at the Paris Artificial Intelligence Research Institute.

Founded in 2012, the ARAMIS Lab, gathering about 35 people, is focused on the design of advanced computational and mathematical approaches to study brain diseases.
